The Industrial Leap: Boldly Building a New Era of Progress

The Age of Industrialization, also known as the Machine Age or the Industrial Revolution, was a period of great technological advancements that changed the world forever. It was a time when machines replaced human labor, and new inventions and discoveries sparked a wave of progress and innovation.

The Industrial Revolution started in Britain in the 18th century and spread to other parts of Europe, the United States, and eventually the rest of the world. It was characterized by the development of new machines, such as the steam engine
